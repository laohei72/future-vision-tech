Our Drone Fleet

A Platform for Every Payload and Mission Profile

Multi-Rotor Series

Our multi-rotor platforms are the workhorses for detailed, close-proximity inspections. Offering superior stability, hovering capabilities, and the ability to carry a wide range of sensor payloads, they are ideal for infrastructure inspection, public safety, and creative industries.

  • Take-off Weight: 500g to 25kg
  • Key Features: Redundant systems, obstacle avoidance, high-precision GPS, and modular payload bays.
  • Common Applications: Wind turbine inspection, power line monitoring, real estate photography, search and rescue.

Fixed-Wing Series

Engineered for endurance and coverage, our fixed-wing UAVs are the ultimate solution for long-range missions. Their aerodynamic efficiency allows for hours of flight time, making them perfect for linear infrastructure monitoring, large-scale agricultural analysis, and beyond-visual-line-of-sight (BVLOS) operations.

  • Take-off Weight: 5kg to 500kg+
  • Key Features: Long flight endurance (up to 10+ hours), high-speed cruise, large payload capacity, and catapult/runway launch options.
  • Common Applications: Pipeline and railway inspection, precision agriculture, large area mapping, border surveillance.

Hybrid VTOL Series

Combining the operational flexibility of a multi-rotor with the endurance of a fixed-wing, our hybrid Vertical Take-Off and Landing (VTOL) platforms eliminate the need for runways. They can operate from confined spaces like ships or forest clearings, then transition to efficient forward flight to cover vast areas.

  • Take-off Weight: 10kg to 150kg
  • Key Features: Fully autonomous transition between hover and forward flight, redundant lift motors for safety, weather-resistant design.
  • Common Applications: Maritime search and rescue, remote mountain inspections, logistics and package delivery.
